Our Vision & Mission

OhamsLight HUB provides online spaces with access to network globally, alongside her community business focused services. OhamslightHub is committed to nurturing individuals not just spiritually, but also in practical skills.

Our vision is to build men and women with a diverse set of talents. Our Vision is to create a world where every lost soul is won, transformed with God's word thereby helping them rediscover God's unfailing Love and Joy amidst the trials in life.

Our Mission is to Build Men with Skill, provide educational resources and mentorship programs for our global youths in Christ.
